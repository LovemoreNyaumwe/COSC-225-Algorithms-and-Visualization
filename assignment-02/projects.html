Mammoth Meals is the official app for the Amherst College campus community, with a focus on dining services, social media and other Amherst services.

Mammoth Meals features:

  • Dining Services menus: Check the menus for Valentine Dining, Grab-n-Go, Frost Café, and Lewis-Sebring. There are also links to a Dining Q&A form and Nutrition Info.
  • Amherst Twitter: View a direct feed of tweets from the Amherst College Twitter account.
  • Event Calendar: Find out what’s happening around campus.
  • Weather: Check to see if you need to bring an umbrella heading out the door.
  • Campus Map: Looking for a particular building around campus? This will definitely help!
  • Social Media: Quick access to Amherst’s Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, and Flickr accounts.
  • Athletics: Find out who won yesterday or when your favorite team plays next. MyAmherst:
  • MyAmherst: Quick access to all the services on MyAmherst (login required). Want more? Let us know! We’ll add new features based on your feedback, so be sure to vote up new tools for the app or use the app store comments to share your thoughts.
